Ongoing Compliance

Ongoing Compliance is the backbone of responsible business management and corporate governance. Agrawal Consulting offers continuous advisory and compliance management services to ensure your business remains aligned with all relevant legal, tax, and regulatory obligations throughout its lifecycle.

Our services encompass monitoring new legislations and regulatory changes, timely filings of annual returns, statutory forms, and all event-based notices with authorities such as the Registrar of Companies, Income Tax Department, and GST department. We prepare and submit required disclosures, certifications, and reports, and conduct health checks and reviews to ensure no compliance gaps exist.

In addition to standard compliance routines, our professionals advise on complex matters including changes in directorship, share capital, buyback, related party transactions, and other special resolutions. We support clients during audits, regulatory inspections, and when responding to government queries or notices.
