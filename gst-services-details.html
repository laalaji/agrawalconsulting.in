GST Services

Our GST Services at Agrawal Consulting offer comprehensive support for all your Goods and Services Tax requirements, ensuring full compliance and peace of mind. We facilitate smooth GST registration for new and existing businesses, advising you on the correct structure and documentation to avoid delays and errors.

Once registered, our team manages regular GST return filings—GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, annual returns, and reconciliations—so that your business remains compliant with the latest statutory timelines and rules. We provide advisory on input tax credit, classification, and practical implications, as well as representation for notices, queries, and audits initiated by the GST authority.

Our approach includes detailed GST health checks and ongoing monitoring of changes in GST laws that may affect your transactions or processes. For businesses undergoing restructuring, mergers, or changes in supply chains, we provide GST impact analysis and compliance roadmaps.

By partnering with Agrawal Consulting, you receive proactive, expert-driven support that streamlines compliance, optimizes tax credits, and helps you avoid costly litigation or penalties.
