Company Secretarial Services

Agrawal Consulting’s Company Secretarial Services ensure that your business remains fully compliant with all statutory and regulatory requirements mandated by Indian corporate law. Our expert team provides end-to-end support in corporate governance, helping your company establish and maintain best practices for board structures, record management, and compliance documentation.

We guide you through the complexities of statutory compliance, including the timely preparation and filing of annual returns, maintenance of statutory registers, and the drafting and recording of board resolutions and meeting minutes. From incorporation and registration of businesses to ongoing company changes—such as alteration in the Board of Directors, registered office, share capital, or business objects—we handle all legal documentation with precision.

Our services also include the preparation and coordination of Annual General Meetings (AGMs), Extraordinary General Meetings (EGMs), and support for secretarial audits. We monitor changes in corporate regulations, ensuring that you are promptly informed and your company remains in line with the latest requirements of authorities like the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Registrar of Companies (ROC), and the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).

With Agrawal Consulting, you gain a trusted partner to minimize compliance risks, avoid penalties, and free your leadership team to focus on your company’s growth and strategy. We ensure your business always operates with transparency, accountability, and in full legal compliance.
