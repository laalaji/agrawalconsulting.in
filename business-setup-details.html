Business Setup & Advisory

At Agrawal Consulting, we believe every successful business starts with a solid legal and strategic foundation. Our Business Setup & Advisory service simplifies formation, registration, and early legal planning for startups, SMEs, and large enterprises.

We advise on the most suitable entity structure—Private Limited Company, LLP, Partnership, Proprietorship, or Section 8 Company—considering compliance, taxation, liability, and ease of scale. Our skilled professionals manage all steps of incorporation, including name reservation, drafting MOA/AOA/LLP deeds, and handling all submissions to the Registrar of Companies and relevant authorities.

Beyond registration, we assist with PAN, TAN, GST, MSME/Udyam certificates, and opening statutory bank accounts. We help new and growing businesses navigate government incentives and regulatory frameworks, so they can establish compliant operations quickly and efficiently.

Our advisory goes beyond launch—providing long-term guidance as your operations evolve, from changes in business model or capital structure to mergers, joint ventures, or exits. Agrawal Consulting’s support helps you mitigate risk and build a strong, future-ready business.
